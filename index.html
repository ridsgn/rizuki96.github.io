Who Am I ?

Hi! I'm Rizky, 23 years old. I was born in Bandung but i grew up in Makassar, South Sulawesi. Raised from Sundanese - Makassar family.

I was student of Vocational High School in Makassar called SMKN 5 Makassar with specialization of computer network. Thanks to Allah coz with that my interest in technology emerged and the first time when i have my 10 inch notebook, i was curios about design, such as manipulating photos, create a vector, silhouette, etc. So, actually i feel my passion is design. I started going into programming thing and excited about it when i'm entered Electrical Engineering study program majoring in electronics, then i signed up into the best robotic community in that college called EPROM -Elektro Poltek Robot Team-. I was programmer in one robot division and tech i used was Arduino, so, C is the first programming language that i knew a little.

After graduate, i want to deepen my programming skills because i don't get much in lectures and suddenly i see a brochure that Nurul Fikri Computer based on Depok was open a scholarship for learning web programming. Then my journey in Depok began.
What i loved about programming is when we have to solve a problem.
